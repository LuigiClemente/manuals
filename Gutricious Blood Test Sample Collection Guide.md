# Gutricious Blood Test Sample Collection Guide

Welcome to your Gutricious blood test! This guide will assist you in collecting and shipping your blood sample efficiently. Follow these steps carefully to ensure accurate results for your health assessment.

### What’s Inside Your Kit

Inside your kit, you will find the following:

**Alcohol Wipe:** For cleaning your finger before sample collection.  
**Finger Prick Needle:** For performing the finger prick and collecting the blood sample.  
**Blood Collection Tube with Cap:** To securely store your blood sample.  
**Plaster:** To cover your finger after the prick.  
**Tissue:** For drying and cleaning your hands.  
**Rigid Safety Bag:** Protects the sample tube during transit.

### Important Reminders

Timing is crucial. Collect your blood sample two hours after consuming your lunch cookies. Ensure you have completed both the breakfast and lunch cookies before proceeding with sample collection.  
Register your shipment by scanning the kit’s QR code to register your shipment online and enable tracking. Confirm that you have the labelled return box ready for shipping.

### Step-by-Step Instructions

#### 1. Preparation

Open the kit carefully and verify that all items listed above are present. Warm your hands by washing them with warm water or rubbing them together to increase blood flow. Select the side of your ring or middle finger for the prick, as these areas are optimal for blood collection. Clean the selected area with the alcohol wipe and allow it to air dry.

#### 2. Collecting Your Blood Sample

Perform the finger prick by twisting off the cap from the finger prick needle. Hold your hand downward and gently press the needle against the side of your finger until you hear a click. Remove the needle after the click to expose the blood.  
Keep your hand pointed downward and gently massage from the palm to the fingertip to encourage blood flow. Fill the collection tube to at least halfway; if additional blood is needed, use a new finger prick needle and repeat the process.  
Secure the sample by closing the cap tightly on the blood collection tube to prevent leaks. Apply the plaster to your finger for protection.

#### 3. Packing Your Sample for Shipping

Secure the sample in the Rigid Safety Bag by placing the capped blood collection tube inside the bag, ensuring it is secured in the plastic holder. Seal the Rigid Safety Bag tightly.  
Place the Rigid Safety Bag in the return box by inserting the sealed bag into the pre-labelled return box. The return box is already labelled for shipping, so no additional labelling is required.

#### 4. Shipping Your Sample

Arrange drop-off or collection by checking the return box for the carrier indicated on the pre-labelled box. Visit the carrier’s website to locate the nearest drop-off location or schedule a pickup, ensuring timely shipping.  
If same-day shipping isn’t possible, store your sample in the refrigerator at approximately +4°C and ship it on the next available collection day.

Thank you for choosing Gutricious for your health journey. Your sample will provide valuable insights into your health. If you have any questions, our support team is ready to assist you.

#### 2. Raccolta del campione di sangue

Effettua la puntura del dito svitando il tappo del dispositivo. Tieni la mano rivolta verso il basso e premi delicatamente il dispositivo sul lato del dito fino a sentire un clic. Rimuovi il dispositivo dopo il clic per far fuoriuscire il sangue.  
Mantieni la mano rivolta verso il basso e massaggia delicatamente dalla base del palmo alla punta del dito per favorire il flusso sanguigno. Riempi il tubo di raccolta almeno fino a metà. Se serve più sangue, utilizza un nuovo dispositivo e ripeti il processo.  
Assicurati che il campione sia ben sigillato chiudendo saldamente il tappo del tubo per evitare perdite. Applica un cerotto sul dito per proteggerlo.

#### 3. Imballaggio del campione per la spedizione

Posiziona il campione nel sacchetto rigido di sicurezza, inserendo il tubo chiuso all’interno del sacchetto e fissandolo nel supporto di plastica. Sigilla accuratamente il sacchetto di sicurezza.  
Inserisci il sacchetto di sicurezza nella scatola di ritorno collocando il sacchetto sigillato nella scatola pre-etichettata. La scatola è già etichettata per la spedizione, quindi non è necessaria alcuna etichettatura aggiuntiva.

#### 4. Spedizione del campione

Organizza la consegna o il ritiro verificando il corriere indicato sulla scatola di ritorno pre-etichettata. Visita il sito web del corriere per trovare il punto di consegna più vicino o pianificare un ritiro, assicurandoti di spedire il campione in tempo.  
Se non è possibile spedire il campione lo stesso giorno, conservalo in frigorifero a circa +4°C e spedisci il giorno lavorativo successivo.
